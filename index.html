Binu John Mathews Music Professional
Vocal Trainer | Performer | Lyricist | Composer

Mr.Binu is a vocal trainer, performer, lyricist and composer by profession. He has been training over 20 leading playback singers in the South Indian music industry. Currently he trains singers from 6 countries. He is well versed in Carnatic music and Western Classical music. He is the lyricist and composer of more than 30 Christian devotionals in Malayalam.

Services


Vocal Training

Provides vocal training services to help people reach their highest potential in singing. My services include voice lessons, vocal exercises, and vocal warm-ups to help improve vocal range, tone, and technique. I also offer advice and guidance on how to properly use the voice, as well as how to maintain vocal health. I have experience teaching a wide variety of vocal styles, and I strive to create a positive and encouraging learning environment for each of my students. With my help, my clients can reach their goals and become the best vocalists they can be.

Keyboard Lessons

Provides piano teaching services to students of all ages and skill levels. With my years of experience and passion for music, I am able to provide a comprehensive and enjoyable learning experience. I am dedicated to helping my students reach their goals and develop their skills in playing the piano. I provide individual and group lessons, as well as practice sessions and workshops. My goal is to help my students become confident and competent in their piano playing.

Choir Workshops

Our choir workshop services provide a unique and engaging opportunity for singers of all ages and skill levels to come together and learn about the fundamentals of music and vocal performance. We offer a variety of workshops that cover topics such as vocal technique, sight-reading, music theory, and performance etiquette. We strive to create a safe and welcoming environment where everyone can develop their musical skills and express themselves through singing.

BIO

Mr. Binu was a member of the faculty at Chetana National Institute of Vocology, Thrissur. He has had a long and distinguished career as a vocal trainer, having worked at Jerry Amaldev Foundation, Kalabhavan in Ernakulam, and MCBS KalaGramam in Trivandrum. He has also collaborated with the renowned Consultant Laryngologist Dr Jayakumar R Menon in voice rehabilitation programs. Currently, Mr. Binu is teaching music at the Choice School, Thiruvalla.
